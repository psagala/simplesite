A Few Words About Me

Hi I am Princess! A student at the University of Northern Colorado, majoring in Business marketing. I am a photographer located in Greeley, Colorado. I have been interested in photography ever since I discovered lightroom. Growing up in Tanzania, I never had those priviledges because I did not own a mobile phone. Coming to the United States gave me an opportunity to discover what I am good at!

Tanzania Interests

Being born in Tanzania. There is wonderful nature and buldings to photograph. One day I want to be able to go to Zanzibar, it is very beautiful and I would love to explore my beautiful country even more. Living in the United States I miss home so much sometimes. I cannot wait the day I go back home and photograph nature, animals, buildings, etc. Check out Tanzania exploring page!

Photography that Interests Me

When I started photography, I never knew what I truly wanted. I was using my phone at the time to take small shots like flowers, sky, and buildings. I was not goot at it of course. I kept wondering what type of photography i would be good at. I kept practicing taking shots of different objects and scenarios. My favorite came to be potrait photography. I fell in love right away. Potrait photography made me capture peoples beauty, emotions, expressions, etc.

More About Me

I am originally from Tanzania, East Africa. I speak two languages Swahili and English. I am a first generation student to attend college. I have a supportive single mother who cares and aknowledges my passion for photography.
